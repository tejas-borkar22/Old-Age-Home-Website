Our Homes And Rooms

Our homes have all the essential features like large well lit bedrooms, elevator access, TV, AC(optional), power-backup, heaters,housekeeping service.
Our homes are carefully selected keeping in mind the requirements, comfort & preferences of our elders. They are then thoughtfully revamped by our design team toadd colour & warmth in addition to making it an elder safe environment.
Our rooms are fully furnished and strong residents are welcome to bring personal belongings and small items of furniture. The bedrooms also feature a colour television with cable TV connectivity and over 100 channels and a staff call bell.
We also have rooms which are suitable for a couple to share.
